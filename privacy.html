Privacy Notice

How we handle data, consent, and advertising.

Data handling

The password generator runs entirely in your browser using the Web Crypto API. No passwords, configuration options, or personal data are sent to our servers. We do not create user accounts and do not maintain server-side logs that can be tied to individual visitors.

We collect only minimal, aggregated telemetry such as page views to understand overall usage. This information cannot be used to identify you and is not combined with advertising data.

Clipboard usage

When you click the Copy button, the generated password is written to your local clipboard through the standard Clipboard API. The password never leaves your device, and we do not receive or store clipboard contents. You can clear your clipboard at any time by copying different text.

Advertising and consent

We participate in the Google AdSense program to fund maintenance of this tool. Personalized advertising is only enabled after you provide consent through our Google-certified Consent Management Platform (CMP). Until consent is recorded, only non-personalized ads are served.

The CMP banner explains how to update your preferences or withdraw consent. You can revisit those controls at any time by selecting the “Privacy & cookie settings” link that appears in the banner and in the site footer.

Ad partners

Our primary advertising partner is Google AdSense, which may rely on Google Marketing Platform and other certified vendors to deliver ads. When you opt in to personalized ads, Google may use cookies or local storage to tailor content. You can review how Google uses data and manage their cookies at Google Ads privacy.
