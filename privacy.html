Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-29

This Privacy Policy explains how the website at secure-password-generator.com handles information when you use the free, client-side password generator. The tool is designed to keep your data on your device, and this document outlines what we do and do not collect.

Data we do not collect

We do not store, log, or transmit any passwords generated by the tool. All generation happens locally in your browser, and nothing you type or create is sent to a server.

Data that may be collected

When you visit the site, standard web server information may be collected automatically, such as IP address, browser and device details, timestamps, and pages visited. This limited data is used only for security, performance monitoring, and basic usage metrics to keep the service reliable.

Third-party services

The site is hosted on Netlify. We may also use optional, privacy-friendly analytics services (such as Netlify Analytics or Cloudflare Web Analytics) that do not rely on tracking cookies. No generated passwords are ever sent to these providers.

Cookies and similar technologies

This site does not use advertising or tracking cookies. If privacy-focused analytics are enabled, they are configured to be cookie-free. Should essential technical cookies be introduced in the future, they will be disclosed in the Cookies page.

Legal basis for processing

For visitors in the EU and UK, we process the limited log data described above under the legitimate interests of operating, securing, and improving the service.

Data retention

Hosting and analytics providers retain server logs only for as long as necessary to provide their services and meet security or legal requirements, in line with their default policies. We do not keep additional copies.

Your rights

We do not collect or store any personal information, generated passwords, or usage data. All password generation occurs locally in your browser. There is no data for us to access, retrieve, or delete.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the site evolves. The latest version will always be posted on this page with the updated date shown at the top.
